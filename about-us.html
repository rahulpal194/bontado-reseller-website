About Bontado

We believe restaurants should own their customers

For years, restaurants have been forced to depend on platforms that control their orders, customer relationships, and margins. Bontado exists to change that. We're building the platform that gives restaurants complete ownership of their online ordering business — while creating new opportunities for agencies and partners to grow recurring revenue.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help restaurants build sustainable, independent businesses by giving them complete ownership of their brand, customers, and revenue.

Sign a restaurant once and earn every month after. Bontado is the white-label ordering platform behind your agency — fully branded to you, with an optional marketplace that turns every client into two income streams instead of one.
